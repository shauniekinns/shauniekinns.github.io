My passion for photography began as I travelled the world, always with a camera in hand. Before digital photography, before smart phones, shots had to be lined up to get the perfect balance of light and drama. One's eye is the best camera of all. I amaze myself that, after all these years, I stand still to gauge the perfect shot. It does not matter where the perfect shot will be. It's there. You just have to have your lens trained to find it.
